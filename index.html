Talking down to your readers has never been easier

Before talkdownify From this simple wikipedia article

There are many species in this genus. Some do prefer places where there is rotting fruit. The adults may feed on nectar, and may lay their eggs on or near decaying fruit. The details differ greatly from species to species. The largest number of species is in the Hawaiian Islands. Some fruit flies can get drunk if they have a gene called "Happy Hour".

After talkdownify

Obviously, there are many species in this genus. Clearly, some do prefer places where there is rotting fruit. Obviously, the adults may feed on nectar, and may lay their eggs on or near decaying fruit. The details differ greatly from species to species. Of course, the largest number of species is in the Hawaiian Islands. Of course, some fruit flies can get drunk if they have a gene called "Happy Hour".

About

I created this sarcastic tool after reading math and computer science texts that talk down to their readers by preceding their conclusions with words like "obviously" and "clearly". I'm not sure why the authors of these texts feel the need to pepper their explanations with these words. It can be frustrating to read an "obvious" result of a theorem or algorithm only to not understand why it's obvious. Obviously, these authors think all their readers think exactly as they do!
