About

I created this sarcastic tool after reading math and computer science texts that talk down to their readers by preceding their conclusions with words like "obviously" and "clearly". I'm not sure why the authors of these texts feel the need to pepper their explanations with these words. It can be frustrating to read an "obvious" result of a theorem or algorithm only to not understand why it's obvious. Obviously, these authors think all their readers think exactly as they do!
